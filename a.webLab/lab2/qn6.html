Popular Places in Nepal

Place 1: Kathmandu

Kathmandu, the capital city of Nepal, is a vibrant and culturally rich destination. It is known for its historic temples, bustling markets, and friendly atmosphere.

Place 2: Pokhara

Pokhara is a picturesque city situated by the tranquil Phewa Lake. It is surrounded by the Annapurna mountain range, offering stunning views and a peaceful ambiance.

Place 3: Lumbini

Lumbini is the birthplace of Lord Buddha and holds immense religious and historical significance. The sacred garden and Maya Devi Temple attract pilgrims from around the world.
